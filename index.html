Whether you live in the vibrant downtown of Belgrade or the city centre of Novi Sad, nature is just a walk away. It is truly the best of both worlds in the cities of Serbia. Kalemegdan Park is a sprawling acreage of forest that contains a huge fortress dating back to the 3rd century BC and breathtaking cliff views of the winding Danube River. You need only cross a small street from the city centre to enter the forests of Kalemegdan filled with promenades, fortress bridges, and breathaking views.
