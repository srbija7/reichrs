Whether you live in a downtown Belgrade flat or a Novi Sad condo, nature is just a walk away. It is truly the best of both worlds in the cities of Serbia. You need only cross a small street from the city center to enter the forests of Kalemegdan filled with promenades, fortress bridges, and breathaking views.
